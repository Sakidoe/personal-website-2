Who We Are

Our Mission: “Provide access to badminton facilities for students to come together, fostering good sportsmanship and leadership skills at all levels”

Our Goals:

Team Bonding

Comaraderie

As a team, we want to create an environment for members to find lifelong friends. From quarterly bonding events, meetups, and many boba trips, we prioritize our members to learn and grow with each other as one team.

Community

Leadership & Etiquette

As part of the badminton community, we want to maintain a healthy relationship with other school clubs. We consistently host meets against schools like SJSU, UCSC, Stanford, and UC Berkeley, and encourage members to attend tournaments.

Victory

Training & Competition

Our vision for the club is to win the National Collegiate Championships. Our weekly practices pair members by level so everyone improves while supporting each other.

Events

fun fun fun fun fun!

Each year, our team holds several events for all members to take part in. Below is a list of our annual events:

Weekly Practices

Mon, Wed, Fri @ Hickey Gym

Open gym practices 3x per week, with courts for different skill levels. Birds are provided and officers supervise play.

School Meets

Home & Away Games

We host multiple meets each quarter, where rosters from different schools face off in competitive matches for points.

Bonding Events

Team Building & Memories

From Six Flags to karaoke nights, our events foster friendships beyond badminton.

Irvine Trip

Annual Tradition

A road trip to Irvine for the collegiate tournament, with coordinated travel, AirBnBs, food, and team bonding.

Collegiate Championships

Boston Nationals

We represent Northern California at the USA Badminton Collegiate Nationals, fighting to defend our regional titles.

Year-End Banquet

Celebration

Each year ends with a banquet to celebrate achievements, with catered food and formal attire.

Davis Open

The Largest Northern California Badminton Tournament, with over 400 entries and 20 events.

Credit Union Center

Stadium Environment

Hosted in the professional Credit Union Center, with 13 courts, lighting, and sound system—perfect for badminton.

Student Ran

Club Coordinated

Organized entirely by UC Davis students, showcasing leadership and event planning skills.

Awards

Trophies & Prizes

Davis-themed trophies, cash prizes, and shirts reward players at the Davis Open.
