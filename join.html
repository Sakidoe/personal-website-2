Joining the Club

Steps.

As fall quarter approaches, we would like to let everyone know the steps for becoming a member for the upcoming 2024-2025 school year! (Open to all current undergraduate and graduate students at UC Davis)

1. UCD Physical Form

Mandatory as of 2023

A completed physical form must be uploaded to DSE by October 24th. It can be completed by your primary care physician, another healthcare provider, or at the UC Davis student health center.

UCD Physical Form Download

2. Membership Dues

Quarterly / Yearly

Membership is $190 per year OR $70 per quarter, payable to our treasurers via Zelle (davisbadminton@gmail.com) or cash by October 6th. Please note dues are non-refundable.

3. Fusion Insurance

Insurance for UC Davis

Insurance is required for all UC Davis Sports Clubs. Log into your UC Davis account, navigate to "Sport Club (Badminton)", and pay the fee. A waiver form is also available.
